## Module Requirements
The following table documents the requirements that I need in order to manufacture, design, and integrate my portion of the system into the larger group assembly. My role is the design and construction of a drill head capable of boring into soil to deposit soil samples to be analyzed by another members system.

| **Requirement Description** | **Measure of<br> Threshold** | **Target<br>Measure** |**Stretch<br>Requirement<br>(Y-N)**|
|-----------------------------| ----------------- | ----------------- | :-----: |
| Surface mounted, 3.3V switching power regulatore | 3.2 Volts | 3.3 Volts | No |
| Surface mounted microcontroller | ESP32 | 8-bit PIC | No |
| Wireless Communication | Able to send or receive a Wi-Fi data | Send and receive Wi-Fi Data to MQTT | Yes |
| Drill head | capable of digging into soil | 4 inches of soil penetration | No |
| 2 12V DC Motors | Able to power drill head, and able to move drill into different locations | 12V | No |
| Motor Driver | Able to engage both motors for forward and backwards action as well as regulate speed | Move both motors with connection to ESP32 | No |
| 12V battery | separate 12V power supply for motor use | Able to power motors for at least 5 minutes of active use | No |
